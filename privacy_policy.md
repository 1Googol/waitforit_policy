# Privacy Policy

This game collects certain information from players in order to provide core gameplay features and online services.

## Information We Collect
1. **SteamID** of the player  
2. **User-submitted content**, including any text or information that players voluntarily enter within the game  
3. This information will be **uploaded to our server** and stored securely to enable gameplay features, maintain user data, and improve the game experience.

## Use of Data
- The collected information is used **only within this game and its related services**.  
- **We do not sell, share, or transfer any collected data to third parties.**  
- No information is used for advertising, analytics tracking, or cross-service profiling.

## Data Security
We implement reasonable technical measures to protect stored data.  
However, no online system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Player Rights
Players may request:
- Access to their stored data  
- Correction of data  
- Deletion of data  

You may submit such requests by contacting us.
